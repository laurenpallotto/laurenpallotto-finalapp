Circle Generator

What is This?

This is a Gui (graphical user interface) that allows you to customeize the number, distance, and stroke weight of of this nine square grid.

How Do I Use It?

  • Circles: "This changes the number of rings on the circles"
  • Distance: "this changes the distance between the circles, starting at their center point"
  • Stroke Weight: "this controles how thick or thin yu want to make lines on the circles"
